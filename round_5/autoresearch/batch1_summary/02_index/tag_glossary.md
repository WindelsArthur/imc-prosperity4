# Tag Glossary

- **mean_reversion** (716): Single-product mid reverts to a fair value (rolling mean, OU, AR, etc.).
- **backtest** (650): Direct backtest result (PnL, Sharpe, drawdown).
- **microstructure** (535): Spread / depth / OFI / queue / imbalance signal at order-book level.
- **basket** (507): Sum / weighted-sum of group members is invariant or near-constant.
- **stat_test** (431): Stationarity / distribution test (ADF, KPSS, KS, Ljung-Box).
- **cointegration** (270): Two products move with stationary residual under linear combination (Engle-Granger or Johansen).
- **ar1** (206): Lag-1 autoregressive coefficient on Δmid.
- **lattice** (197): Mid takes a small number of distinct values (quasi-discrete lattice).
- **intraday** (174): Time-of-day pattern (mod-K) in returns or activity.
- **sine** (106): Deterministic sine fit of price level over the stitched 30k-tick training window.
- **lead_lag** (79): Cross-correlation peaks at non-zero lag between two products.
- **regime** (26): HMM / Kalman / GARCH-style regime-switching model.
- **pair** (26): Pair-finding (two products jointly).
- **ml** (2): ML-distilled (Lasso, GBM, RF) signal.
